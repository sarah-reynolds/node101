Races of Skyrim

High Elf

The Altmer, or High Elves, are a race of elves that inhabit parts of Tamriel. They are one of the ten default playable races, primarily known for their enhanced magical abilities.

  • +10 Illusion
  • +5 Alteration
  • +5 Conjuration
  • +5 Destruction
  • +5 Enchanting
  • +5 Restoration

Special abilities

  • Highborn: Regenerate magicka faster for 60 seconds.
  • Highborn: High Elves are born with 50 extra magicka.
